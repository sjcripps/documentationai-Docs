## Overview

EzBiz Services automates your entire front office, handling calls, texts, scheduling, follow-ups, reviews, and reporting. You focus on delivering great service while the system captures every opportunity and prevents lost revenue from missed calls. Designed specifically for service businesses like plumbers, electricians, and HVAC technicians, it ensures no lead slips away—even when you're on the job or off the clock.

Service businesses lose thousands monthly to unanswered calls: 62% go unanswered, and 85% of voicemail callers never call back. EzBiz changes that with instant responses and intelligent automation, starting from just $97 per month.
