## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid phone number for your service business
- Access to your phone carrier account (for porting or forwarding)
- An email address for account verification
- Stripe account for billing (optional during trial)
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for EzBiz Services to access your automated front office dashboard. The process takes under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Go to [https://dashboard.ezbizservices.com](https://dashboard.ezbizservices.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your business name, email, and phone number. Create a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and access the setup wizard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Run the Initial Setup Wizard

The setup wizard guides you through essential configurations. Follow these steps to get live in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Plan" icon="credit-card">
    Choose **Starter** (`$97/mo`) or higher. Start with the 30-day trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Business Info" icon="settings">
    Enter your service area, hours, and greeting message. Example: "Thanks for calling Ez Plumbing, how can we help?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up SMS Responses" icon="message-circle">
    Enable auto-text-back for missed calls: "Sorry we missed you! We'll call back in 15 minutes."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your Phone Number

Route calls to EzBiz Services by forwarding or porting your number.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Call Forwarding" icon="phone">
    From your carrier account:
    
````bash
*72 +1-601-552-5990  # Forward all calls (US carriers)
*73                    # Disable forwarding
````
    
    Test by calling your number — hear the AI receptionist.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Port Your Number" icon="upload">
    In the dashboard:
    
    1. Go to **Phone Numbers > Port In**
    2. Enter your number and carrier details
    3. Submit — live in 24 hours
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Porting keeps your existing number; no need to update marketing materials.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configure Notifications and SMS

Set up real-time alerts for calls and messages.

<CodeGroup tabs="Dashboard Config,API Config">
  ```json
  {
    "notifications": {
      "sms_enabled": true,
      "email_enabled": true,
      "webhook_url": "https://your-webhook-url.com/ezbiz"
    },
    "sms_template": "New lead from {caller_name}: {message}"
  }
  ```
  ```javascript
  // POST to https://api.ezbizservices.com/v1/notifications
  const config = {
    sms_enabled: true,
    webhook_url: 'https://your-webhook-url.com/ezbiz/webhook'
  };
  
  fetch('https://api.ezbizservices.com/v1/notifications', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: { 'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY' },
    body: JSON.stringify(config)
  });
  ```
</CodeGroup>

<ParamField header="Authorization" param-type="string" required="true">
  Bearer token: `Bearer YOUR_API_KEY`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="sms_template" param-type="string" required="false">
  Custom SMS response, e.g., "Thanks {caller_name}! We'll reply soon."
</ParamField>

## Test Your Setup

Make a test call to your forwarded number. You should receive:
- SMS alert with caller details
- Dashboard notification
- Auto-text-back to the caller

<Callout kind="success">
  Success! Your automated front office is live. Expect to recover missed calls immediately.
</Callout>
