## Overview

To begin using EzBiz Services:

1. Visit `https://dashboard.ezbiz.com`
2. Log in or sign up for a free account
3. Click "Create Project" on the dashboard
4. Fill in your project name and details
5. Click "Submit" to finish setup

Your new project is now ready and listed on your dashboard. Open it to access documentation and management tools.
