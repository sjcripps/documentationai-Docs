## Overview

Projects help you organize documentation in EzBiz Services.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Log in at `https://dashboard.ezbiz.com` and navigate to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click the "Create Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in your project name and description. Submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Project">
    Your project appears in the list. Click to begin managing docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
