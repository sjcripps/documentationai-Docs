---
title: Can't Log In
description: Troubleshoot EzBiz dashboard login problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to access EzBiz dashboard
- Login screen reloads or shows error

## Causes

- Incorrect email or password
- Browser cache issues

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot password?" and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Open browser settings and clear cache/cookies. Try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
