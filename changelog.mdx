## Recent Updates

<Callout kind="info">
EzBiz Services releases updates regularly to enhance automation for calls, texts, scheduling, and reporting. Check this changelog for new features, bug fixes, and migration guides.
</Callout>

<Update label="2024-11-15" description="v1.3.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Added AI-powered review requests: Automatically send review links after completed jobs via SMS.
- Enhanced appointment scheduling: Integrate with Google Calendar for real-time availability syncing.

## Improvements

- Reduced call handling latency by 40% for faster customer responses.
- Improved text-back personalization using caller history.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-10-20" description="v1.2.1" tags={["bugfix", "improvement"]}>

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed missed call notifications not triggering during peak hours.
- Resolved scheduling reminder duplicates for multi-location businesses.

## Improvements

- Updated reporting dashboard with export to CSV and PDF formats.
- Better handling of international phone numbers in SMS flows.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-09-10" description="v1.2.0" tags={["feature", "breaking"]}>

## New Features

- Introduced follow-up automation: Auto-schedule check-in texts 48 hours post-job.
- Added real-time reporting API for custom integrations.

## Breaking Changes

- Deprecated legacy SMS gateway; migrate to new `smsProvider` config option.
- Updated webhook payload format for call events—review your endpoints.

</Update>

## Upgrade Instructions

EzBiz Services updates deploy automatically for most users. Follow these steps for any manual configurations or breaking changes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Changelog" icon="book-open">
    Check the tags above for `breaking` changes affecting your setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Configuration" icon="settings">
    Log in to your dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com` and verify settings.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="Dashboard,API">
    ````json
    {
      "smsProvider": "new-gateway",
      "webhookUrl": "https://your-webhook-url.com/webhook"
    }
    ````
    ````bash
    curl -X PATCH https://api.example.com/v1/config \\
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \\
      -d '{"smsProvider": "new-gateway"}'
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Integrations" icon="zap">
    Make a test call to `(601) 552-5990` and confirm notifications and scheduling work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Dashboard" icon="activity">
    Watch reports for 24 hours post-upgrade to ensure smooth operation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
For v1.2.0 users, update your webhook handlers by November 30, 2024, to avoid disruptions.
</Callout>
